Group

Brinkman International Group, Inc. is a privately held, corporation headquartered in Rochester, NY. Our corporation takes pride in a decentralized structure with six operating companies located in New York and Florida. Two of our companies, Davenport Machine and CJWinter Machine Technologies manufacture proprietary product lines which include Davenport screw machines, thread roll attachments, thread rolls, and specialty dies. Our remaining four companies, Brinkman Precision, MSK Precision, Gates Albert, and Brinkman Defense. are fully integrated contract manufacturers.
Brinkman Precision

Brinkman Precision, Inc. specializes in complete program management for the aerospace, medical and oil and gas industries. From complex machining of single components to complete assemblies, quality is the key to our success. Core competencies include CNC grinding, match grinding, turning, TIG welding, Swiss-style turning, internal heat treating and brazing. Brinkman Precision specializes in difficult to machine materials such as Inconel, L605, Hast-X, and Monel. Complex tight tolerance components are our specialty.

Brinkman Defense

Brinkman Defense was founded in 2017 to specialize in the manufacturing of military-grade precision metal parts. Brinkman International Group has been serving the needs of direct and indirect defense industry clients, such as the DOD and Tier 1 suppliers, since World War II. Brinkman Defense's focus is on creating products that conform to the most current military standards. From proper material usage to manufacturing to packaging, Brinkman Defense combines the sole focus of an individual operation with the level of quality and precision expected of any member of the Brinkman International Group family.

Davenport Machine

Davenport Machine manufactures Davenport Screw machines, tooling and genuine Davenport replacement parts. With our legendary Davenport Model B and the precision oriented Davenport HP, Davenport continues to prove that there is no more efficient way of producing large volumes of small, precision parts than on a Davenport. From Aerospace fasteners to plumbing and electrical connectors, manufacturing companies around the world continue to rely on Davenport Machines to get the job done quickly and profitably.

MSK Precision

MSK Precision, Inc. specializes in precision parts manufacturing and assembly for the medical, telecommunication and aerospace industries. With a packaging and assembly department, MSK is capable of providing individual parts or complete finished assemblies. Core competencies include precision Swiss-Style turning, ultra-precision cutter grinding, CNC milling, CNC turning, laser marking, and passivation. Complex medical and aerospace components are our specialty.
